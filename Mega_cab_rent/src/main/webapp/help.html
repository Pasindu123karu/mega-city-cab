Customer Help - Mega City Cab

Getting Started

Welcome to Mega City Cab! Follow these simple steps to book your ride:

  • Register/Login: Sign up or log in securely with your credentials.
  • Book a Cab: Enter your pickup location, destination, and preferred ride time.
  • View Booking Details: Check your confirmed rides and current ride status.
  • Print/Download Bill: View and download your ride invoice.
  • Logout: Ensure you log out after usage to keep your account secure.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the steps to reset your password.

2. Can I modify my booking?

No, you will need to cancel your existing booking and create a new one.

3. How is my fare calculated?

The fare is based on distance, time, and applicable taxes or discounts.

4. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our 24/7 support team via the contact page or call our helpline.
